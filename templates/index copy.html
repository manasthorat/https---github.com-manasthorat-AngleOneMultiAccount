Getting Started

Welcome to the Angle One Multi-Account Trading System. This system allows you to automate trading across multiple Angle One accounts using TradingView webhooks.

To get started:

  1. Configure your Angle One accounts in the Accounts section
  2. Configure TradingView alerts to send webhooks to your server
  3. Monitor your orders and positions in the dashboard
